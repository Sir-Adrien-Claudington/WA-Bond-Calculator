## Full Description (for PWA store listings)
StarScape transforms your desktop browser into a window on the universe. Using real astronomical data from ESA's Hipparcos catalogue and NASA's JPL Horizons, it renders an accurate 3D starfield of 187 bright stars and tracks the live positions of Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, and the Moon.

Scroll through mythological stories of Orion, Ursa Major, Scorpius, Cygnus, and Leo — each constellation brought to life with original text and procedurally generated star line art. Check tonight's sky conditions with real-time cloud cover data from Open Meteo.

All data is public domain or openly licensed. No tracking, no cookies, no registration required.

**Features:**
- Immersive Three.js WebGL starfield with scroll-driven camera drift
- 187 bright stars from the ESA Hipparcos Catalogue (public domain)
- Live planet positions from JPL Horizons (NASA/JPL-Caltech, public domain)
- Sky conditions: cloud cover, visibility, limiting magnitude (Open Meteo CC BY 4.0)
- Five constellation mythology cards with scroll-reveal animations
- Fully accessible: screen reader support, reduced-motion respected
- PWA-ready: installable on desktop

## Privacy Policy Summary
StarScape collects no personal data. The geolocation API is used solely to fetch local sky conditions — coordinates are sent to Open Meteo and JPL Horizons and are not stored. No analytics, no cookies, no accounts.
