Privacy Policy

wabond.today — last updated 7 June 2026

Overview

The WA Rental Bond Claim Calculator (wabond.today) is a free, browser-based tool. All bond calculations run entirely in your browser and no calculation data is ever transmitted to any server.

The only personal information we collect is an email address, and only if you choose to export a PDF of your calculation results.

What we collect

When you request a PDF export, you are asked to provide your email address. We collect:

  • Your email address
  • The date and time of the request

We do not collect:

  • Any bond calculation inputs you entered (rent, deductions, etc.)
  • Your name, address, or any other personal details
  • IP address (not logged by the application)
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers

How we use your email

Your email address is used to send you relevant updates about Western Australian tenancy law, including changes to the Residential Tenancies Act 1987 (WA) and associated regulations. We will not send promotional material, sell your address, or share it with third parties.

How your data is stored

Email addresses are stored in a secure database hosted on Railway (a cloud hosting provider). Data is stored within their infrastructure and is not shared with other organisations.

Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your email address at any time
  • Unsubscribe from all emails at any time

How to unsubscribe or request deletion

To unsubscribe, click the unsubscribe link in any email you receive from us, or reply to any of our emails with “Unsubscribe” in the subject line. To request deletion of your email address, reply to any of our emails with “Delete my data” in the subject line. We will action your request within 7 business days.

Analytics

This site does not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other third-party analytics service. Cloudflare may collect aggregated, anonymised traffic statistics (page views, country of origin) as part of its DNS and proxy service. Cloudflare does not share this data with us at the individual level.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the calculator after any change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, reply to any email you have received from us. If you have not received an email from us, use the unsubscribe or deletion mechanism above — we will respond within 7 business days.

This tool provides a general estimate only and does not constitute legal advice. For disputes, contact Consumer Protection WA or the State Administrative Tribunal.

Rules are based on the Residential Tenancies Act 1987 (WA) and the Residential Tenancies Amendment Act 2024 (WA).
